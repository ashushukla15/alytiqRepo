    Dr Sunil Dwivedi is a Senior Consultant Cardiologist (Interventional Cardiologist) based in Bangalore, with a thriving experience of over 20 years in the field. As a dedicated professional, he is utterly passionate about his profession and is always ready to serve his patients. 

     

    He specialises in Intervention Cardiology and is an expert at performing Coronary, Peripheral and Acute Coronary Syndrome Interventions (PCI) and Peripheral Interventions (IVC Filter Insertion). 

    ​

    Currently working as the Senior Consultant cardiologist (Interventional Cardiologist) at Manipal Hospital, Bangalore, he has played an instrumental role in establishing the clinic. Over the years, Dr Sunil Dwivedi has earned a strong reputation and has been recognised as one of the finest cardiologists in the city. He is completely dedicated to what he does and continues to strive every day to bring excellent results for his patients.

    ABOUT HOSPITAL

    At Manipal Hospital, we collectively aspire to provide you with the compassionate healthcare you rightfully deserve. Our tailored services are meticulously crafted to deliver the most suitable care for your individual requirements.

     

    Our superlative cardiac experts handle the most delicate of cardiac cases across all ages. Their range of treatment expertise includes the entire spectrum of cardiac conditions including congenital heart disease, coronary artery disease, heart rhythm disorders, disorders of heart failure, and valvular disease. 

     

    Best in their speciality, our team of eminent echo cardiologists, electrophysiologists, interventional cardiologists, radiologists and cardiovascular surgeons are skilled in treating the most challenging of heart conditions including paediatric surgeries even in newborn. You can avail the assistance of Bangalore best Cardiologist at Manipal.
